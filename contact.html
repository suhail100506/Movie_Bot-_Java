Frequently Asked Questions

How does MovieBot's recommendation system work?

MovieBot uses advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze your viewing history, ratings, and preferences to suggest movies you're likely to enjoy. We combine collaborative filtering with content-based analysis for the most accurate recommendations.

Is MovieBot free to use?

Yes! MovieBot's basic features are completely free. You can browse movies, get recommendations, and read reviews without any cost. We also offer premium features for enhanced personalization.

Where can I watch the recommended movies?

We partner with legal streaming platforms like Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, and others to show you where each movie is available to stream or rent legally.

How can I improve my recommendations?

The more you interact with MovieBot - rating movies, adding to your watchlist, and updating your preferences - the better our recommendations become. We learn from your feedback!
